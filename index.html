About District Studio

Hey there! This is District Studio, and honestly, we're just getting started. Two friends who met making random Roblox scripts decided to team up and build something cool together.

Our first game is still very much a work in progress, but we're excited about what we're creating. Think creative building meets strategy, with a healthy dose of "let's see what happens when we try this crazy idea."
